Frequently Asked Questions

What Saskatchewan business owners and operators ask before starting a custom software project.

We serve a wide range of Saskatchewan industries — trades and construction, healthcare, professional services, agriculture, logistics, and multi-location service businesses. If your business has a workflow problem that off-the-shelf tools haven't solved, we want to hear about it.
Off-the-shelf tools are built for the average business. Custom software is built for your business. It follows your workflow, uses your terminology, and fits your team's process. Over time it's also more cost-effective than stacking multiple SaaS subscriptions that partially cover your needs.
Smaller tools and single-workflow automation take 4–8 weeks. Mid-size projects like a CRM or multi-user business system take 8–16 weeks. Larger enterprise builds are scoped individually. We give you a firm timeline upfront after the discovery phase — not a vague estimate.
We work with businesses across all of Saskatchewan — Saskatoon, Regina, Prince Albert, Moose Jaw, and rural operations province-wide. Custom software is built and maintained remotely, so geography doesn't limit what we can build or support for your team.
Most projects start at $10,000 for simpler tools and range to $50,000+ for full operational systems. We scope every project individually and give you a firm quote after a free discovery call. No surprises, no scope creep without your explicit sign-off first.
