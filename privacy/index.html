Privacy Policy

GrowSK Tech is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us with questions.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a form on our website, we collect the information you provide — including your name, business name, email address, phone number, website URL, and any message you include. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and provide the services you requested.

We may also collect basic analytics data such as pages visited, time on site, and geographic region through standard website analytics tools. This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to individual identities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to inquiries and audit requests
  • Provide and improve our digital marketing services
  • Send project updates, proposals, and service-related communications
  • Understand how visitors use our website to improve it

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Form Submissions and Third-Party Processors

Contact forms on this website are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted to Formspree and then forwarded to us by email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We may also use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some website features may not function correctly without them.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by applicable law. If you would like your information removed from our records, please contact us at the email address below.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate information, or request deletion of your information. To make any of these requests, contact us at hello@growsktech.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we follow industry-standard practices to protect the information we collect.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of those changes.
